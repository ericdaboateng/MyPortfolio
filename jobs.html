With this charity, I was tasked as part of their media department to create a blog posting app that could be fully regulated and maintained by the charities admin team. Based upon the charities specification, they required

• User based system, Different user levels, Articles that can be viewed and posted by users
• Password resets and email notifications
• Header images can be uploaded with the article
• Users having the ability to comment on other users articles
• Users are able to edit their own articles.
• Articles posted by standard users are first reviewed and approved by admin users before being published on the site.
• Admin users are able to remove articles from public view, delete and edit articles including removing comments made to the article.
